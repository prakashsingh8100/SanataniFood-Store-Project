Frequently Asked Questions

~Do you offer vegan recipes?

Absolutely! We have a wide selection of vegan recipes that exclude all animal products, including dairy. Look for the "vegan" label on our recipes.

~ What kind of ingredients do your recipes use?

Our recipes use a variety of fresh, wholesome ingredients, including grains, lentils, vegetables, fruits, nuts, and spices. We prioritize seasonal and locally sourced ingredients whenever possible..

~Are all recipes on Sanatani Foodies vegetarian?

Yes, all our recipes are strictly vegetarian, adhering to the principles of ahimsa (non-violence) and traditional dietary practices of Sanatana Dharma.

~ Do you provide nutritional information for your recipes?

Yes, we provide nutritional information for many of our recipes. This includes calorie count, macronutrient breakdown (carbohydrates, proteins, fats), and other relevant nutritional details.
